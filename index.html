About us.

Prolific Gardens is a community driven organization committed to organic gardening. Our goal is to make growing vegetables, fruits, and herbs in your own backyard is affordable, sustainable, and simple. Organic gardening can be fun, therapeutic, and educational (especially for the youngsters). It can also be labor intensive and require some know-how.

This is where we fit in. Leave the labor, logistics, and know-how to us, while we transform a portion of your lawn into a thriving organic garden. Our all natural approach to sustainable gardening is a refreshing change of pace. At Prolific Gardens we believe the way we grow our food matters and our team is here to help get back to our roots, through every garden we build.

It is always a good day to start a garden.
