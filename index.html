A quick tagline here.

Prolific Gardens is dedicated to bringing its customers an affordable, sustainable, and organic approach to growing produce. We provide sustainable agriculture solutions by creating and maintaining home gardens tailored to fit your needs. Our mission is to make fresh, organic produce available by providing a service that will design and install raised garden beds as well as maintain your garden from the plants down to the soil.

An important part of our business model is that we do not use any pesticides. Instead, crop rotation, intelligent planting practices and healthy soil is our approach to pest mitigation. Northern Virginia's climate is ideal for cultivating many vegetables, fruits, and herbs. Although our winters can bring a drastic drop in temperatures, innovative methods allow us to keep our customers harvesting through the seasons. Our goal is to deliver a year round garden to table option that is both local and healthy. We looking forward to growing with you.
