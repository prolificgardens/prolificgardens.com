Organic, Sustainable, Local

We are getting back to our roots by offering our customers a traditional yet innovative approach to putting food on the table. Prolific Gardens is a Northern Virginia based company dedicated to changing the landscape of local food access. Grow with us and transform a portion of your yard into a thriving year round vegetable garden today.

Installation

During an on site consultation we determine the best location for your garden. Based on your preferences we design a garden that fits your goals. The next step is the garden installation. This involves importing topsoil into your new cedar raised beds, automated drip irrigation, stone walking paths between garden beds, and more.

Maintenance

Once the installation process has been completed we have no intention of leaving you with a garden to maintain unless you so desire. We visit your garden on a regular basis and examine the overall well being of your garden. From there we will add soil amendments accordingly to keep your crops healthy and vibrant. Also, our team will trim plants, sow new seeds to keep your crops producing, and check for any pest infestations. It is important to note that Prolific Gardens does not incorporate any kind of pesticides or unnatural treatments of pest mitigation into your garden. We instead rely on healthy soil to boost the immune system of your crops and crop rotation to fool pests.

Year to Year

Sit back, relax, and let us handle the year to year operations that keep your garden active during each season. We plan and implement crop rotations in order to naturally mitigate pests in your garden. Our team will regularly plant your crops during each season while performing routine soil amendments. Leave the planning and logistics of gardening up to us. Simply watch your crops grow and enjoy the harvest!

Get to know us.

At Prolific Gardens, our mission is to offer a turnkey approach to home gardening. We want our customers to have fresh, organic produce available to them in a setting where they can be apart of the process by having the farm in their backyard. Gardening is fun and time consuming. This is why we have shaped our business around the customer. Not every customer is the same. Some may wish to be more hands on while another may not have the time to tend to their garden. In either case we are here to help.

Combined (knowledge / experience) from the landscape construction industry and organic gardening methods have blended to create a unique venture. Our primary focus is providing the customer with a garden that is prolific and at the same time pleasing to the eye, because after all a garden is apart of the landscape of your home.

At the end of the day our belief structure is deeply rooted in providing our families with the highest quality organic produce that is free from harmful pesticides and toxins. Ultimately this concept is the reason the company was founded and resonates in each step we take when dealing with our clients. Throughout the process we work with our customers to provide a gardening solution that is conscious of both the health of the environment and our loved ones.

We look forward to growing with you.
